\textbf{Constraint Programming} \cite{HoeveK06} is a powerful paradigm that offers a generic and modular approach to modeling and solving combinatorial problems.
In the wider field of artificial intelligence, constraint programming stands out by expressing problems in terms of variables, domains, and constraints.
This declarative approach allows for reusable models and leverages advanced constraint propagation and search techniques to efficiently explore solution spaces.
